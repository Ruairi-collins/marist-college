Marist College is a secondary school for boys in Athlone, County Westhmeath, Ireland.

The school was founded in 1884 by the Marist Brothers, a Freanchorder, and the first principle was Brother Mungo. The original school was based in Glesson Street/Saint Mary,s Square. The collage had a seminary for Juniorate traning brtween 1915 and 1936 located at Bailieborough castle,County Cavan.

In 1973, the school moved to its current location on Retreat Road. The current principal is Michael Dermody and the deputy principal is Paul Kelly.
